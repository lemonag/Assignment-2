About Me

Ainsley Lemon is a current student at Miami University. She is majoring in Emerging Technology Business and Design with the hope to pursue a future career in social media marketing. Originally from Columbus, Ohio, Ainsley is enjoying exploring the sights of Oxford. She enjoys partaking in many hobbies including painting, drawing, and digital art. In addtion, she loves taking long walks and exploring nature. Ainsley also enjoys caring for her collection of houseplants which lives in her dorm room window. She has collected over twenty different kinds of houseplants from succulents to snake plants. Her current favorite houseplant is the Echeveria, commonly known as the "Snow Angel," due to its bluish green color. In the future, she hopes to open a plant shop to spread her love of houseplants and nature to others in her community.
